Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a game to my collection? Go to the Search tab, type the game name, tap the result, and use "Add to Collection" or "Add to Wishlist."
How does the AI Rules Assistant work? Open any game in your collection, tap "Rules Assistant," and ask any question about that game's rules, setup, or strategy. The AI already knows which game you're looking at.
How do recommendations work? The app learns from your ratings and play history. The more games you rate, the better the recommendations become — both for you individually and for game night groups.
Is my data stored online? No. All your collection data, ratings, and player profiles are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.
How do I restore my subscription? Go to the Rules Assistant, tap the subscription prompt, and use "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.
