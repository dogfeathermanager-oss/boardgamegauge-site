Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

Overview

The Board Game Gauge ("the App") is a board game collection tracker and rules assistant. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Stored on Your Device

All of your game collection data, player profiles, game sessions, ratings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to our servers.

AI Rules Assistant

When you use the AI Rules Assistant, your questions are sent to a server to generate responses. These messages are not stored after the response is generated. No conversation history is retained on our servers. The AI service is provided via Google Gemini through a secure proxy.

BoardGameGeek Data

The App fetches publicly available game information from the BoardGameGeek (BGG) API. No personal data is sent to BGG.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks. We do not collect usage analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
